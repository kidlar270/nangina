Word from the Principal

A Tradition of Academic Brilliance

Welcome to St. Cecilia Nangina Girls. For over 60 years, we have stood as a beacon of hope and excellence in Busia County. Our philosophy is simple: we believe every girl has a unique potential that needs the right soil to bloom.


Our students don't just excel in national exams; they lead in the National Science Congress, dominate the Music Festival stages, and captain sports teams that compete at the regional level.
