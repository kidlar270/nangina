Welcome to Nangina Girls

Excellence in Education & Character

At St. Cecilia Nangina Girls Senior School, we nurture young women to achieve academic excellence while developing leadership, creativity, and integrity. Our dedicated faculty ensures a holistic education blending academics, sports, and spiritual growth.

Mission: To empower girls with knowledge, confidence, and values for lifelong success.

Vision: To be a leading institution shaping future leaders in Busia and beyond.
